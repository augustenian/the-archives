THE AUTHOR

ERIN is is 24 years old, nonbinary (they/them pronouns), and working in health IT. This site is their place to bring their stories into the world, available for public consumption, without the hassle of trying to be published.

(They would like to have at least one book published, though. Someday.)

Support them by buying them a coffee here, or just help retweet and/or spread their work on the world wide web.
